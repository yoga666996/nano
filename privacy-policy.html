When you upload banana images to our Google Nano Banana AI platform:

Our Google banana AI service automatically collects:

  • Error reports and debugging information
  • Analytics data about service usage

We use your personal data and banana images for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide Google Nano Banana AI editing services and process your banana pictures
  • Improvement: To enhance our nano-banana AI model performance and consistency
  • Communication: To send updates about our Google banana AI service
  • Support: To provide customer support and technical assistance

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We may share your data with trusted third-party services that help us operate Google Nano Banana AI:

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Google Nano Banana AI, our users, or others.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your banana images and personal data:
